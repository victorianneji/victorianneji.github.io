--- title: "Simulator of Human Operator Workload (SHOW)" excerpt: "A discrete event simulation designed to model vehicle operator workload.
" collection: portfolio --- The Simulator of Human Operator Workload (SHOW) is a tool to simulate a freight rail operator's workload across the duration of a given trip. With SHOW, you can choose a trip with unique conditions and then see the operator's average workload after thousands of similar trips.
